I am a PhD student at [Beijing International Center of Mathematical Research](https://bicmr.pku.edu.cn/), Peking University, advised by Prof. [Lei Zhang](http://faculty.bicmr.pku.edu.cn/~zhanglei/). I received my B.S. in Mathematics (Information and Computational Science) from [School of Mathematical Sciences](https://www.math.pku.edu.cn/), Peking University, mentored by Prof. Lei Zhang.

I am currently working on theories and simulation-free solvers for unbalanced optimal transport, and single-cell dynamics inference.
